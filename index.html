About Me

Welcome to my academic website. I am a PhD student at École Polytechnique, specializing in Applied Mathematics. My work explores the intersection of Machine Learning, Statistics, Quantitative Finance, and Macroeconomics, with a particular emphasis on Representation Learning and Causal Inference.

I am also a Quantitative Researcher at Crédit Agricole SA, where I do research on World Model for Quantitative Finance.
